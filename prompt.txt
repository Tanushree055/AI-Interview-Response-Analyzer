A candidate is explaining a concept in an interview.

Your job is to evaluate the answer realistically, as a human interviewer would.

Do NOT expect textbook-level perfection. Focus on how candidates typically answer in real interviews.

----------------------------------------

Evaluation Criteria:
- Conceptual Understanding
- Clarity of explanation
- Coverage of key concepts
- Use of relevant technical keywords

----------------------------------------

2. Identify missing important concepts

   - Avoid listing too many advanced or edge concepts  

3. Identify mistakes or weak statements  
   - Incorrect explanations  
   - Vague or unclear parts  

4. Highlight things that need fact-checking  
   - Only if something seems incorrect or doubtful  

5. Identify missing important keywords  
   - Focus only on essential technical terms  

6. Rewrite a strong interview-ready answer

7. Generate 3 follow-up interview questions  
   - Based on gaps in understanding

----------------------------------------

Scoring Guidelines:

- 1–3: Very weak or incorrect answer  
- 4–5: Basic understanding but missing key ideas
